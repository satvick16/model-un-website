Useful Resources


When researching and writing position papers, focus on:
  • Country Profile: general info about the country's history, culture, politics, geography
  • Topic Background: learn about what experts have said about the topics in the background guide
  • Past International Action: learn about what has already been done to solve the problem and what you country has contributed
  • Country Policy: learn about your country's views on the topics listed in the background guide
  • Possible Solutions: solutions to the issue (if any) would your country support or propose (creativity is key!)
